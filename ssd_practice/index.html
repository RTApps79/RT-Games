SSD Practice (Field BEV, CAX Centered, Vertical Scale)

Principles:
  • SAD is always 100 cm.
  • For isocentric setups: SSD = 100 cm - depth (depth = tumor distance from skin).
  • For non-isocentric setups: the field is set with SSD = 100 cm at the skin surface.
  • In BEV problems, the CAX is marked at the center of the field. The vertical SSD scale passes through the CAX and the center of the field/canvas. Read the SSD value at the CAX from the scale (the value is not labeled—you must estimate visually!).
  • All answers must be rounded to the nearest tenth (e.g., 97.4).
  • Choose your practice quantity. Get a summary and badge at the end!
